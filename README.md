# GEOMETRIC-TRUTH

This is not fanancial advice.

A technical and legal research framework analyzing systemic risks in AI governance, financial valuation, and algorithmic accountability.
The purpose of this repository is documentation, clarity, and structured analysis. It does not assert conspiracy, propose ideology, or prescribe authority. It serves as a research body for review, critique, regulatory consideration, and academic discussion.

Scope

This repository organizes materials that explore:

AI system dependency and operational control claims

Financial valuation risks tied to third-party AI reliance

Systemic fragility and feedback dynamics in digital infrastructure

Constitutional considerations regarding human authority vs. machine output

Legal accountability standards related to disclosure, control, and liability

Documentation integrity and traceability in AI-driven systems
